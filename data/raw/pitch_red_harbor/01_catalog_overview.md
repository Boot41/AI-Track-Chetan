# Catalog Overview: Red Harbor Collection

## Catalog Details
- **Catalog Name:** Red Harbor Collection
- **Content Mix:** 52 titles (40 features, 12 limited series/documentaries).
- **Genres:** Action, Drama, International Indie, Documentary.
- **Regions of Strongest Value:** Europe (France, Germany, UK), Asia (Japan, South Korea, India), North America.
- **Likely Audience Appeal:** Gen Z and Millennials (International/Indie focus), Gen X (Mid-Tier Action).

## Strategic Rationale
1. **Volume at Scale:** Provides a significant boost to the platform's content library for a relatively low upfront cost.
2. **Global Diversity:** Fills a critical gap in the "International Indie" and "Mid-Tier Action" sub-genres, appealing to underserved regional audiences.
3. **Efficiency:** Offers a high volume of content for a single, consolidated license fee, reducing operational overhead for multiple individual deals.

## Positioning Statement
"The Red Harbor Collection is a curated, global library for the modern cinephile. From award-winning international indies to high-octane action thrillers, it's the perfect addition for a platform that values diversity and volume."

## Estimated License Fee
- **Total Acquisition Cost:** $15M - $20M for the Initial Term.
- **Cost Per Title:** ~$300k - $400k (High efficiency for the volume).

## Conclusion
The Red Harbor Collection is a strategic acquisition for a platform looking to expand its global content library and appeal to diverse audience segments. While some titles may require additional localization, the overall volume and variety provide significant value and ROI potential.
