## Expected Signals

### Narrative
- **Positives:** Diverse content mix with strong international appeal; several award-winning indie titles.
- **Red Flags:** Some older titles may have "dated" production values; a few niche titles have very limited audience appeal.

### ROI
- **Upside:** High volume of content for a relatively low license fee; potential for "long-tail" viewership.
- **Downside:** Significant localization costs for some international titles; high maintenance/storage costs for a large catalog.

### Risk
- **Legal:** Exclusivity windows for several key titles are complex and overlapping.
- **Regulatory:** Several titles may face censorship challenges in specific MENA/SEA regions due to political or societal themes.

### Catalog Fit
- **Positives:** Fills gaps in "International Indie" and "Mid-Tier Action"; strong appeal for underserved regional audiences.
- **Negatives:** High overlap with existing "Action" sub-genre in the US market.
