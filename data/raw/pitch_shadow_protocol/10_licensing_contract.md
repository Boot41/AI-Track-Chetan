# Licensing Contract: Shadow Protocol

## 1. DEFINITIONS
- **"Content":** The 8-episode original series titled "Shadow Protocol" (Season 1).
- **"Platform":** The Licensee's OTT streaming service.
- **"Territory":** Worldwide (Excluding China, for which separate negotiations are required).
- **"Initial Term":** 5 years from the date of the first episode's release.
- **"GCRM":** Global Content Rights Management.

## 2. GRANT OF RIGHTS
GCRM hereby grants to the Licensee the exclusive right to exhibit, transmit, and perform the Content on the Platform throughout the Territory during the Initial Term. This includes the right to:
- **2.1. Dubbing and Subtitling:** Create and distribute localized versions of the Content in all languages of the Territory.
- **2.2. Merchandising:** Develop and sell physical and digital products related to the series.
- **2.3. Derivative Works:** Develop, produce, and exhibit spin-offs, prequels, and sequels, subject to Section 14.3.

## 3. TERRITORY RESTRICTIONS
- **3.1. Cultural Review:** Licensee acknowledges that the depiction of fictional government entities may require specific cultural review and potential edits for distribution in certain MENA and SEA regions. 
- **3.2. Compliance:** Licensee is responsible for ensuring the Content complies with all local laws and regulations in the Territory.

## 4. EXCLUSIVITY WINDOWS
- **4.1. Global Exclusivity:** Worldwide exclusivity for the Initial Term. No other OTT or linear distribution permitted during this period.
- **4.2. Carve-outs:** GCRM retains rights for educational/museum exhibition and film festival screenings.

## 5. SUB-LICENSING PROVISIONS
Licensee may sub-license the Content to third-party OTT platforms in the Territory with prior written consent from GCRM, which shall not be unreasonably withheld. 

## 6. DUBBING/SUBTITLING RESTRICTIONS
- **6.1. Quality Standards:** All dubbing and subtitling must meet GCRM's high-quality standards and be subject to GCRM's approval.
- **6.2. Technical Accuracy:** All technical jargon (e.g., "Protocol 9") must be accurately localized.

## 12. INDEMNITY & LIABILITY
- **12.1. Licensor's Representation:** GCRM represents and warrants that it owns all necessary rights to the Content.
- **12.2. Licensee's Indemnity:** Licensee shall indemnify and hold GCRM harmless from any claims arising from the Licensee's use or distribution of the Content.

## 14. MISCELLANEOUS
- **14.3 MATCHING RIGHTS:** If the Licensee wishes to develop a spin-off, sequel, or prequel to the Content, GCRM has the right to match any third-party offer for the same rights. If GCRM matches the offer, the Licensee must negotiate in good faith with GCRM for the development and distribution of the new series.
- **14.5 CONDITIONAL APPROVAL:** The Licensee's right to exhibit the Content in certain MENA and SEA regions is conditional upon the Licensee's successful completion of the cultural review and adaptation process, as specified in Section 3.1. 

## 15. TERMINATION TRIGGERS
- **15.1. Non-Payment:** Failure to pay the license fee within 30 days of the due date.
- **15.2. Material Breach:** Any significant breach of the Grant of Rights or Exclusivity provisions.
- **15.3. Key Talent Departure:** The departure of the lead actress (Elara Vance) or the lead writer (TBD) may trigger a termination review at the discretion of the Licensee.

## Conclusion
This contract for "Shadow Protocol" is a comprehensive and binding agreement. The Matching Rights and Cultural Compliance clauses are of particular significance for the long-term success of the series and the Licensee's platform.
