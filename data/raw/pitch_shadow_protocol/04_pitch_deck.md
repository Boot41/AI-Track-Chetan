# Pitch Deck: Shadow Protocol

# Slide 1: Shadow Protocol
*The world's most dangerous secret isn't hidden. It's in the code.*

# Slide 2: The Concept
"Shadow Protocol" is a 8-episode cyber-noir thriller. It's "Mr. Robot" meets "The Night Manager." A high-stakes chase through the digital and physical underworld.

# Slide 3: Audience Positioning
- **Primary:** Gen Z and Millennials (18-35).
- **Interests:** Tech, privacy, high-concept thrillers, prestige drama.
- **Positioning:** The next "must-watch" binge series for the digital generation.

# Slide 4: Why Now?
In an era of AI, data breaches, and mass surveillance, "Shadow Protocol" taps into the collective anxiety of the digital age. It's timely, relevant, and visually stunning.

# Slide 5: The Comps
- **Mr. Robot:** For its technical accuracy and paranoid tone.
- **The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo:** For its gritty noir aesthetic and brilliant female lead.
- **Hanna:** For its high-stakes action and "on the run" tension.

# Slide 6: Regional Opportunity
- **Europe:** High demand for sophisticated thrillers (Germany, UK).
- **Asia:** Strong interest in tech-driven narratives (Japan, South Korea).
- **Americas:** Huge potential in Brazil and Mexico for high-prestige originals.

# Slide 7: Visual Tone
- **Cyber-Noir:** Neon lights, rain-slicked streets, brutalist architecture.
- **Analog Contrast:** Grainy film looks for "offline" scenes, providing a tactile feel.

# Slide 8: Budget Framing
- **Per Episode:** $6M average.
- **Total:** $48M.
- **Allocation:** High-end visual effects, international locations (London, Berlin, Tokyo), and a top-tier cast.

# Slide 9: Awards Potential
- **Visuals:** Emmy-worthy cinematography and VFX.
- **Lead Actress:** A career-defining role for a rising star.
- **Writing:** Sharp, intelligent, and socially relevant.

# Slide 10: Franchise & Beyond
- **Global Expansion:** Spin-offs in major tech hubs.
- **Interactive Experience:** Deep-dive AR lore and "analog drops."
- **Merchandising:** High-end tech and "off-grid" gear.
