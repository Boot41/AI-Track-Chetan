# Rights Term Sheet: Shadow Protocol

## Parties
- **Producer:** Shadow Productions, LLC (a subsidiary of High-Tier Content Group)
- **Licensor:** Global Content Rights Management (GCRM)
- **Licensee:** The Platform (Decision-Support Application Owner)

## Rights Granted
1. **Exclusive Streaming Rights:** To exhibit, transmit, and perform the 8-episode Season 1 of "Shadow Protocol" on the Licensee's OTT platform.
2. **Derivative Rights:** Rights to develop, produce, and exhibit spin-offs, prequels, and sequels, subject to the Matching Rights clause (Section 14.3).
3. **Merchandising Rights:** Rights to create and sell branded physical and digital products related to the series.
4. **Localization Rights:** Rights to dub, subtitle, and culturally adapt the series for all licensed territories.

## Territories
- **Licensed Territories:** Worldwide (Excluding China, for which separate negotiations are required).
- **Territory Restrictions:** Depiction of fictional government entities may require specific cultural review/edits for distribution in certain MENA and SEA regions.

## Term Length
- **Initial Term:** 5 years from the date of the first episode's release.
- **Renewal Option:** Licensee has the right to renew for an additional 3-year term, subject to a 10% increase in the license fee.

## Exclusivity
- **Exclusive Window:** Worldwide exclusivity for the Initial Term. No other OTT or linear distribution permitted during this period.
- **Carve-outs:** GCRM retains rights for educational/museum exhibition and film festival screenings.

## Termination Conditions
- **Licensee Default:** Failure to pay license fees or breach of non-compete clauses.
- **Licensor Default:** Failure to deliver high-quality master files or breach of representations and warranties.
- **Material Change:** Significant changes to the creative team (e.g., departure of the lead actress) may trigger a termination review.

## Key Clauses for Review
- **14.3 Matching Rights:** If the Licensee wishes to develop a spin-off, GCRM has the right to match any third-party offer for the same rights.
- **12.5 Cultural Compliance:** Licensee is responsible for all costs related to cultural adaptation and regulatory compliance in MENA and SEA.

## Conclusion
The proposed rights deal for "Shadow Protocol" is comprehensive but contains several key clauses that require careful review. The Matching Rights and Cultural Compliance provisions are of particular concern for long-term franchise development and international distribution.
