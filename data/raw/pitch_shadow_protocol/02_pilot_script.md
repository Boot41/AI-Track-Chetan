# Pilot Script: Shadow Protocol
## Episode Title: "The Ghost in the Ledger"

## Scene 1 - INT. NSA DATA HUB - NIGHT
BLUE LIGHT from rows of servers hums. ELARA VANCE (29, sharp, weary eyes) is the only one in the room. Her fingers fly across a holographic interface. 

ELARA
(muttering)
Where are you, you little glitch? 

A flickering red dot on the screen. It doesn't move. It pulses. 

ELARA
Protocol 9. You're not supposed to exist.

A door slides open. ARTHUR (50, stern, NSA Director) enters.

ARTHUR
Vance. You're still here. 

ELARA
Arthur. Just cleaning up some... digital lint. 

ARTHUR
Leave the lint. Go home. That's an order.

## Scene 2 - EXT. RAIN-SLICKED STREETS - CONTINUOUS
Elara walks through a neon-lit alley. She's being followed. A MAN IN A GREY COAT stays twenty paces behind. 

Elara ducks into a coffee shop. 

## Scene 3 - INT. COFFEE SHOP - NIGHT
Elara sits at a corner table. She opens her laptop. 

MAN IN GREY COAT
(standing over her)
The Protocol isn't lint, Elara. 

Elara doesn't look up. 

ELARA
I know. It's a bomb.

MAN IN GREY COAT
And you just lit the fuse.

## Scene 4 - INT. ELARA'S APARTMENT - LATER
Elara is frantically packing. Her apartment is filled with "retro" tech—typewriters, analog cameras. 

ELARA
(to herself)
If they can't track it, they can't find it. 

The lights flicker. A message appears on her screen: **"ACCESS DENIED. USER IDENTITY RECONCILED."**

Elara's phone rings. It's a block number. 

VOICE (O.S.)
Your bank account is zeroed, Elara. Your passport is void. You don't exist anymore.

## Scene 5 - EXT. ROOFTOP - NIGHT
Elara stands on the edge of a high-rise. Below, the city is a grid of light. 

ELARA
(whispering)
I'm a ghost now. And ghosts can haunt.

She jumps—to the next building. A narrow escape. 

## Scene 6 - INT. UNDERGROUND SERVER ROOM - NIGHT
Elara meets KAI (25, hyperactive, hacker). 

KAI
You're Elara Vance? The 'Oracle of Langley'? 

ELARA
I was. Now I'm just Elara. Can you get me into the Consortium's backbone?

KAI
It's a suicide mission. 

ELARA
Then let's go together.

## Scene 7 - INT. CONSORTIUM HEADQUARTERS - NIGHT
High-tech, sterile. The "Shadow Protocol" is being initialized. 

LEADER (O.S.)
Execute the Protocol. Let the world burn, then we build it back in our image.

## Scene 8 - INT. SERVER ROOM - CONTINUOUS
Elara is hacking. Suddenly, her screen goes black. 

ELARA
No! 

A red flag: Elara's screen shows a code she doesn't recognize. Is it a trap? Or a deeper level of the Protocol? 

ELARA
(frustrated)
The quantum decryption is failing! The entropy is too high!

## Scene 9 - EXT. HARBOR - NIGHT
Elara and Kai are on a boat. 

KAI
We lost them. 

ELARA
For now. But they have the key. 

## Scene 10 - INT. HIDDEN BUNKER - NIGHT
Elara looks at a photo of her father. 

ELARA
You knew, didn't you? You knew the Protocol was coming. 

She finds a hidden compartment in the frame. A physical key. 

ELARA
The analog solution to a digital problem. 

## Scene 11 - EXT. CITY SKYLINE - DAWN
Elara watches the sun rise. The first episode ends with a massive digital blackout across the city. 

ELARA
Let the haunting begin. 

## Scene 12 - INT. MYSTERY ROOM - CONTINUOUS
A mysterious figure watches Elara on a monitor. 

MYSTERY FIGURE
She has the key. Prepare the extraction. 

The screen fades to black. 
**[END OF PILOT]**
