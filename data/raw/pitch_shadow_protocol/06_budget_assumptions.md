# Budget Assumptions: Shadow Protocol

## Total Production Budget (8 Episodes)
- **Estimated Range:** $48,000,000 - $56,000,000
- **Average Per Episode:** $6,500,000

## Major Line Items (Per Episode)
| Category | Allocation | Notes |
|----------|------------|-------|
| Above-the-Line (Cast/Writer/Director) | $1,200,000 | Core talent and key creative roles. |
| Production Crew & Gear | $2,000,000 | Location-based filming, high-end equipment. |
| Visual Effects (VFX) | $1,500,000 | Substantial "near-future" tech and digital environments. |
| Locations & Logistics | $800,000 | International filming in London, Berlin, and Tokyo. |
| Post-Production (Editing/Sound) | $500,000 | Complex sound design and scoring. |
| Contingency (10%) | $500,000 | General unforeseen costs. |

## Marketing Assumptions
- **Total Marketing Budget:** $15,000,000
- **Strategy:** 
  - **Pre-Launch (40%):** Digital teasers, influencer partnerships, and AR "analog drops."
  - **Launch Phase (40%):** High-impact TV/Streaming ads, large-scale outdoor advertising in key tech hubs (San Francisco, London, Berlin).
  - **Post-Launch (20%):** Sustained digital presence, community management, and award season campaigning.

## Localization Assumptions
- **Total Localization Cost:** $3,000,000
- **Dubbing (10 Regions):** $1,500,000 (Tier 1: US, UK, Germany, France, Japan, South Korea, etc.)
- **Subtitling (20+ Regions):** $500,000 (Broad global coverage)
- **Cultural Adaptation/Review:** $1,000,000 (Crucial for tech/regulatory nuances in MENA and SEA).

## Financial Scenario Analysis
- **Optimistic Scenario:** 
  - **Viewership:** 25M+ households in Season 1.
  - **Retention Lift:** High due to strong "binge-ability" scores.
  - **Estimated ROI:** 2.5x (including merchandising and spin-off potential).
- **Base Scenario:**
  - **Viewership:** 15M households.
  - **Retention Lift:** Moderate.
  - **Estimated ROI:** 1.5x.
- **Conservative Scenario:**
  - **Viewership:** 10M households.
  - **Retention Lift:** Low.
  - **Estimated ROI:** 0.8x (Breakeven risk).

## Financial Strategic Rationale
The high production and marketing costs are justified by the "prestige" nature of the project. A successful cyber-thriller can drive substantial platform adoption and retention among a high-value, younger demographic. The localization strategy is designed to maximize global reach while carefully managing costs in Tier 2 regions.
