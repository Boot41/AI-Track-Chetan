## Expected Signals

### Narrative
- **Positives:** High-stakes "ticking clock" pacing, morally grey protagonist with strong arc, unique "near-future" tech aesthetics.
- **Red Flags:** Complex terminology in Scene 4 may cause audience confusion; Act 3 pacing slows significantly before the climax.

### ROI
- **Upside:** High retention potential due to episodic cliffhangers; strong "binge-ability" score in focus groups.
- **Downside:** High production cost for "near-future" visual effects; marketing budget heavily weighted toward US/UK only.

### Risk
- **Legal:** Clause 14.3 (Matching Rights) creates a potential blocker for future spin-offs.
- **Regulatory:** Depiction of fictional government data breaches may trigger censorship in specific MENA/SEA regions.

### Catalog Fit
- **Positives:** Fills a gap in "Mature Thriller" for 18-35 male demographic; complements existing "High-Tech" sub-genre.
- **Negatives:** Overlap with "The Grid" (existing series) might cannibalize its viewership in the first month.
