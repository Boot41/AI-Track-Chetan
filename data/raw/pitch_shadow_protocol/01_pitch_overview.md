# Pitch Overview: Shadow Protocol

## Project Details
- **Title:** Shadow Protocol
- **Format:** 8-episode limited series (Original)
- **Genre:** Cyber-Espionage / Noir Thriller
- **Logline:** When a disgraced NSA analyst discovers a global conspiracy hidden in plain sight within the world's financial systems, she must go off-grid to expose the "Shadow Protocol" before her digital footprint is erased forever.

## Synopsis
In a near-future where every transaction and heartbeat is tracked, ELARA VANCE, a genius but cynical analyst, finds an anomaly: "Protocol 9". It's a dormant code that can redirect global wealth in seconds. Elara realizes the code isn't a bug—it's a weapon. 

Pursued by a shadow organization known as "The Consortium," Elara must navigate a world of shifting alliances, high-tech heists, and digital ghosts. The series follows her journey from a buttoned-up analyst to a hardened digital insurgent, questioning the cost of transparency in a world addicted to secrets.

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** Adults 18-35 (Tech-savvy, thriller fans)
- **Secondary:** Fans of "Mr. Robot" and "The Night Manager."

## Target Regions
- **Tier 1:** US, UK, Canada, Germany, France, Japan, South Korea.
- **Tier 2:** Brazil, Mexico, Australia, India.

## Positioning Statement
"Shadow Protocol" is a gritty, realistic look at the vulnerabilities of our digital age. It combines the intellectual depth of a high-tech thriller with the visceral pacing of a classic noir. It positions the platform as the home for "prestige cyber-noir."

## Financial Overview
- **Estimated Budget Range:** $5M - $7M per episode.
- **Total Production Budget:** $40M - $56M.

## Strategic Rationale
1. **Fills Content Gap:** Currently, the platform lacks a high-end cyber-thriller that appeals to a younger, male-leaning demographic.
2. **Franchise Potential:** The world of Elara Vance is ripe for spin-offs and expanded lore.
3. **Global Appeal:** Tech-driven thrillers have consistently high viewership in key international markets (Germany, Japan).
