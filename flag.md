
  User Test Prompts


  A. Narrative Parsing
   1. "Analyze the narrative pacing of 'Shadow Protocol'. Are
      there any mid-pilot drop-off risks?"
   2. "What are the core themes in the 'Red Harbor' catalog, and
      how do they support franchise expansion?"
   3. "Describe the character arc for the protagonist in 'Shadow
      Protocol' and cite evidence from the pilot script."
   4. "Does the 'Red Harbor' overview suggest any spin-off
      potential for the Asian market?"


  B. ROI/Viewership Projection
   1. "What is the projected completion rate for 'Shadow
      Protocol' based on its genre and similar titles?"
   2. "Calculate the estimated ROI for acquiring 'Red Harbor'
      given its historical performance in Europe."
   3. "If we increase the production budget for 'Shadow Protocol'
      by 20%, how does the cost-per-view change?"
   4. "Show me the retention lift assumptions for 'Red Harbor'
      compared to our current top-tier drama."


  C. Risk/Regulatory/Contract Analysis
   1. "Identify any territory restrictions in the 'Red Harbor'
      licensing contract that conflict with our expansion in
      India."
   2. "Are there any exclusivity windows in 'Shadow Protocol'
      that would prevent a simultaneous global launch?"
   3. "Flag any censorship risks in the 'Shadow Protocol' pilot
      script for Middle Eastern markets."
   4. "Does the 'Red Harbor' term sheet include matching rights
      for future seasons?"


  D. Catalog Fit Scoring
   1. "How well does 'Red Harbor' fill our current gap in the
      young-adult thriller demographic?"
   2. "Compare 'Shadow Protocol' against our existing sci-fi
      catalog. Is there too much overlap?"
   3. "Which underserved audiences in Latin America would 'Red
      Harbor' most likely attract?"
   4. "Given the high churn in our 18-24 segment, does 'Shadow
      Protocol' offer a strong strategic fit?"


  E. Contextual Conversation
   1. "Why was the risk score for 'Red Harbor' so high? Show me
      the specific clauses." (Follow-up)
   2. "Compare the ROI of greenlighting 'Shadow Protocol' versus
      acquiring the 'Red Harbor' catalog."
   3. "What if we only acquire the digital rights for 'Red
      Harbor' instead of the full library?"
   4. "Tell me more about the 'pacing red flags' you mentioned in
      the previous answer." (Session-state reuse)


  F. Structured JSON Contract
   1. "Provide a full greenlight scorecard for 'Shadow
      Protocol'." (Verify scorecard and evidence fields)
   2. "Generate an acquisition summary for 'Red Harbor' including
      all evidence citations."
   3. "What is our recommendation for 'Shadow Protocol'? Ensure
      the output includes confidence metrics."


  G. Failure/Uncertainty Behavior
   1. "Should we greenlight 'Project X'?" (Where Project X does
      not exist in the corpus - test low confidence/missing
      details)
   2. "Compare 'Red Harbor' with 'The Midnight Show'." (Where
      'Midnight Show' has no data - test partial comparison)
   3. "Provide a risk analysis for a contract I haven't uploaded
      yet." (Test incomplete context handling)
